Welcome to Culture Wise Living!

Do you live and study or work abroad? Have you ever felt angry for no reason, or sad? Have you ever made someone else angry or sad without meaning to, without even understanding what just happened?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, then this blog is for you.

I love intercultural communication and have lived, studied and worked with people from all over the world. I love the moment when things go wrong and you take a deep breath, look at the situation and try to figure out what just happened. When you share your life with people from different cultures you learn as much about your own expectations, beliefs and assumptions as about their culture. In this blog I will share what I have learned about intercultural communication over the years, living and travelling on two continents in the hope that it will help you understand what might be happening when things go wrong.

If you want to know a bit more about me, check out my About page. If you want to jump right into it, you can start by reading my Top Tips, read up on culture and culture shock, or dive right into the blog posts.
